Who We Are

Navarca Ship Management LLC is a UAE-based vessel operator providing commercial and operational solutions for clients in the offshore, energy, and logistics sectors. Whether owned or chartered, every vessel under our control is fully crewed, technically maintained, and contractually ready for deployment. We work with trusted ship managers and deliver assets ready for work.

What We Do

  • Vessel Operations

    Navarca operates vessels under long-term charters, ensuring readiness, compliance, and full alignment with client schedules and operational needs.

  • Asset Deployment

    We deploy high-performance landing craft and support vessels—owned or chartered—as required by project scope, location, and draft restrictions.

  • Performance Oversight

    We partner with specialized technical managers and oversee all aspects of crew, maintenance, and safety to deliver consistent uptime and service quality.
